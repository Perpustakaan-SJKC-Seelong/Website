Pusat Sumber SJK (C) Seelong History

Pusat Sumber SJK (C) Seelong is the school's resource center, serving as a hub for educational materials and activities. It supports both students and teachers by providing access to a variety of learning resources, thereby enhancing the teaching and learning experience. The center aims to foster a culture of reading and self-directed learning among students.

For a glimpse into the activities and programs conducted by the Pusat Sumber, you can visit their YouTube channel, TVPSS SJKC Seelong.
